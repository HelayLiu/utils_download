- As a token holder, I want to transfer tokens to other addresses securely within the same chain.
- As a cross-chain user, I want to send tokens to other chains using LayerZero's infrastructure while maintaining state isolation.
- As the contract owner, I want to configure trusted peers and message inspectors to ensure secure cross-chain communication.
- As a spender, I want approved token allowances isolated to prevent unauthorized access to user balances.
- As a recipient, I want to receive tokens from other chains with guarantees of amount preservation and origin authentication.
- As a security auditor, I want message inspection capabilities to validate cross-chain payloads before execution.
